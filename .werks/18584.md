# Filters: Service filters renamed

edition    | community

The "Service (exact match)" and "Service (regex)" filters are renamed to
"Service name (exact match)" and "Service name (regex)" respectively.
This change was done to improve searchability of these filters
and to align them with the "Host name" filters.

This is purely a cosmetic change and does not affect the functionality of the
filters.
