# Improve notification rule rendering

key        | value
---------- | ---
date       | 2026-03-03T15:16:52+00:00
version    | 2.4.0p25
class      | fix
edition    | cre
component  | notifications
level      | 1

In installations with many notification rules, the loading times of the
notification page could be very long. Even saving notification rules could lead
to an error (502).

Additionally, in large setups the notification pages could be very slow due to
redundant permission loading and repeated user list computation. Both are now
cached per request.
