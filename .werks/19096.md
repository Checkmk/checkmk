# Fix timeout in alert timeline dashlet during DST transitions

key        | value
---------- | ---
date       | 2026-03-16T15:13:43.834237+00:00
version    | 2.6.0b1
class      | fix
edition    | pro
component  | multisite
level      | 1
compatible | yes

Previously, the alert timeline and notification timeline dashlets timed out after 110 seconds
when their time range included the hour of a DST spring-forward transition (e.g. the last
Sunday of March in Europe). During fall-back transitions, the repeated hour was silently skipped.

Both issues are fixed. The dashlets now render correctly across all DST transitions.
