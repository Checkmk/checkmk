This Werk is a followup to Werk #19120 affecting Cisco CW9800M devices.

Client monitoring was inadvertently excluded from the previous Werk.

This new Werk adds the OID to the `wlc_clients` check plugin so that client
counts are now monitored as well.

The new services will appear upon discovery on the pertient Cisco CW9800M
devices.
