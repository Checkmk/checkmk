# azure_v2 storage accounts: lower default levels for used capacity

The default levels for the Azure storage account _Used capacity_ service have
been lowered. Previously, WARN was at 50 TiB and CRIT at 500 TiB. The new
defaults are 1 TiB (WARN) and 5 TiB (CRIT).

## Impact

Services that were previously reporting OK may now report WARN or CRIT after
upgrading. If you want different thresholds, configure the ruleset
_Azure Storage Accounts_ with your preferred levels.

The ruleset's prefill values for new rules have also been updated to match
these new defaults.
