# Preserve search query after editing notification rule

In the notifications view, the filter now remains applied after you edit and save a notification. Previously, the filter was lost upon saving, which forced users to reapply it and disrupted their workflow.

This improvement makes it easier to manage multiple notifications without repeatedly setting the filter. The change is automatic, so you do not need to adjust your usual process.
