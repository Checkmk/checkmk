# nimble_latency: Fix KeyError['total']

The check plugins `nimble_latency`  and `nimble_latency_write` could crash with `KeyError: 'total'`.

[Werk #7918](https://checkmk.com/werk/7918) originally guarded against exactly this situation.
But the protection was inadvertently lost in [Werk #11330](https://checkmk.com/werk/11330).

Volumes without latency data are no longer discovered.
