Checkmk SaaS now ships with an AI assistant that helps you understand
and act on what your monitoring is telling you, without leaving Checkmk.
The feature is available on Checkmk SaaS / Cloud only.

## How to use it

When a service is in a concerning state, click _Explain with AI_.
Two entry points are available on every service:

* a sparkle icon in the _Icons_ column of any service listing, including
  the _Services of host_ view and generic service overviews, and
* an _Explain with AI_ button on each individual service's page.

A side panel slides in and the answer streams in progressively, so you
can start reading right away.

## What you get back

Each answer assess what is happening on the service, how serious it is and puts it into context by also considering the overall state of the host. It suggests concrete next steps to investigate or fix the issue.

## Activation and permissions

The feature is controlled by two switches:

* Global setting _Enable AI-based explanations_ (default on). Disabling
  it hides both entry points and prevents any data from being sent to
  the AI service.
* Role permission _AI: Explain with AI_ (default on for the `admin` and
  `user` roles). Revoke it to hide the feature from a role.

The first time you use the feature you are shown a legal disclaimer
naming the AI provider currently powering the answers. Your
acknowledgement is remembered per user in the browser.

## What data is sent

To produce a useful answer the assistant forwards a snapshot of the
relevant monitoring data to an external AI provider. This snapshot
covers the host and service you clicked on together with the other
services running on the same host.
