# Restore broken 'select all' toggle on Setup pages

Previously, the small "X" toggle button at the top of each table on the
service discovery page — and on several other Setup pages, including
rulesets, users, folders, and BI configuration — was supposed to
(de-)select every checkbox in its table at once, but clicking it had no
effect.

This has been fixed. The button now toggles all checkboxes in its
table as expected.
