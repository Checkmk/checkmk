# Service discovery: Remove all and find new now removes vanished services

The service discovery action _Remove all and find new_ (also known as _Tabula rasa_) did not remove vanished services from the autochecks file.
This was inconsistent with the action's label and with the equivalent _Refresh all services and host labels (tabula rasa)_ option used by bulk discovery and periodic discovery, which both remove vanished services.

The bug was particularly visible for vanished services that are also matched by a _Disabled services_ rule: such services kept reappearing on the discovery page after clicking _Remove all and find new_.

The interactive action now removes all vanished services, including those matched by a _Disabled services_ rule.
