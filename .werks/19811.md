If you monitor Redfish devices that use newer BMC firmware, you now get
performance data for all sensor types — not just temperature and humidity.
This means graphs and thresholds for:

- Fan speed (RPM or %)
- Voltage (V)
- Current (A)
- Power consumption (W)
- Frequency (Hz)

## New services

Three new services are available for newer Redfish devices:

- _Power Subsystem_ — power supply redundancy status and total capacity
- _Environment_ — aggregate system power consumption
- _Power supply_ — individual PSU details like manufacturer, model, and capacity

These services appear automatically during discovery on supported devices.
If your devices were already monitored before this update, nothing changes.
